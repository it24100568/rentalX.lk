FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How Can We Help You?

Find answers to the most common questions about our rental services. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact our support team.

How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service. To book online, simply select your desired vehicle, rental dates, and location. Follow the prompts to complete your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your reservation.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation through your account dashboard or by contacting our customer service. Modifications are subject to vehicle availability. Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours may be subject to a cancellation fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and digital payment methods like PayPal and Apple Pay. For security reasons, the credit card used for the reservation must be presented at the time of pickup along with a valid ID that matches the name on the card.

Is a security deposit required?

Yes, a security deposit is required when you pick up the vehicle. The amount varies depending on the vehicle type and rental duration. The deposit is typically held as a pre-authorization on your credit card and is released after the vehicle is returned in the same condition as when it was rented.

What happens if the vehicle breaks down?

All our vehicles are regularly maintained to ensure reliability. However, if you experience a breakdown, call our 24/7 roadside assistance number provided in your rental agreement. We'll arrange for assistance and, if necessary, a replacement vehicle at no additional cost to you.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between our locations. Additional fees may apply depending on the distance between pickup and drop-off locations. One-way rentals must be arranged in advance to ensure availability at the destination location.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
